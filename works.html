Our Work

Community Awareness Programs

Through our community awareness programs, we educate people about the sources and effects of air pollution. We organize workshops, seminars, and awareness campaigns to empower communities to take action against air pollution.

Green Initiatives

We support and promote green initiatives aimed at reducing air pollution. This includes tree planting drives, promoting renewable energy sources, and advocating for sustainable transportation methods.

Research and Advocacy

We conduct research to understand the causes and impacts of air pollution. Through our advocacy efforts, we work with policymakers and stakeholders to develop and implement effective policies and regulations to combat air pollution.
